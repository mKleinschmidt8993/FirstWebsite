Lately, the game I have been most interested in would be Hadrian's Wall. It is a game that look's very complicated upon first glance, but after further explomation can be broken down into a few parts:

Defense

The primarly part of the game is about defending the wall, it keeps you from losing points (disdain) and can earn you valor (which will convert to points at the end of the game)

Resource Production

Gaining resources is critical to being able to building the wall and completing anything with the merchant tracks. You begin with the 8 resources on the starting card and then check your resource tracks for other resources you begin with (everyone begins with one stone).
